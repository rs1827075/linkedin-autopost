## Post text (edit freely, this is what gets published)

I have been exploring the potential of using Hosted MCP Servers to bridge Slack and Salesforce, specifically through the Headless 360 approach. This setup aims to consolidate your workspace by providing a unified conversational interface directly inside Slack. Instead of context switching between platforms, teams can now securely interact with CRM data, trigger automation flows, and query Tableau insights without leaving their messaging app. For those of us working in Financial Services Cloud, this could streamline how advisors access client records or run automated tasks during a conversation, potentially saving significant time in daily workflows. It simplifies how we interact with our backend systems, moving toward a more fluid and conversational way of managing complex data. I am curious to see how others are planning to leverage this integration to reduce friction for their users. How do you see these conversational interfaces changing your current integration strategy? Let me know your thoughts or use cases in the comments. #Salesforce #Slack #Integration #FinancialServicesCloud #DeveloperExperience
